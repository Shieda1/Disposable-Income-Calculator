What is the disposable personal income calculator?
The disposable income calculator is a tool that calculates how much income is left after familial or individual obligations to the government. How to calculate disposable personal income?
To calculate disposable personal income you should use the following formula:
disposable personal income = personal income - government taxes + government transfers
